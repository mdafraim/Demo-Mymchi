INTRODUCTION

Formed in 1982, CREDAI-MCHI is one of the premier housing and developers associations in India. The CREDAI-MCHI Kalyan-Dombivli unit is representive body of progressive developers formed in 2010 and is operating in one of the fastest growing region of MMR, encompassing the Kalyan-Dombivli unit region.

THE CREDAI - MCHI KALYAN - DOMBIVILI UNIT CHAPTER HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED
TO ACHIEVE THE FOLLOWING OBJECTIVES
  • To make the Kalyan-Dombivli unit the most preferred destination for affordable and mid income housing through organized, planned and balanced development.
  • To foster and forge unity, co-operation and mutual support amongst its members.
  • To create a platform for interaction with the regulatory bodies and government agencies on policy matters relating to the development of this region.
  • Adopting an ethical code of conduct for members.
  • Propagating best-in-class industry practices amongst its members
