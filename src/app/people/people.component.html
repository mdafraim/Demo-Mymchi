MR SHRIKANT SHITOLE
PRESIDENT, CREDAI MCHI-KDU

Mr Shrikant Shitole, the Chairman and Managing Director of Tycoons Realties, is a visionary entrepreneur with innovative thoughts and with principles of ethics, transparency, developing good human network, creating values in whichever activities he takes up has enhanced the brand ‘Tycoons’ in the industry.

Mr Shitole is Graduate from Commerce, also holds Intermediate degree from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and Institute of Cost & Works Accounts of India (ICWAI). After academic accomplishments he worked with Hindustan Lever Ltd. He started his enterpreneurial journey in the year 2000. Mr. Shitole is a first3 generation entrepreneur. He envisioned the luxury need of kalyan and constructed the first city club of Kalyan “ Springtime Club” one of the elite properties in entire region. Springtime is a family club with patrons from all walks of life and frequented by the ‘Who’s Who in the city. Apart from the Springtime, Mr Shitole has been instrumental in developing and delivering mixed use real estate projects to the tune of nearly One million sqft and 3.5 million sq ft under development in the heart of the city under the banner of “Tycoons Group” with focus on quality construction, efficient designs. Quality and Consumer satisfaction is always on his mind which has taken the group at a notch above the competition and having very high brand recall in short span of time.

Mr Shitole has been instrumental in forming CREDAI MCHI KDU on 31st March 2010 under the leadership of Mr Sunil Mantri & Rajan Bandelkar from Mumbai unit and Bandish Ajmera as founder president, Mr Shitole was Founder-Secretary for 5 years and has served as Vice-president, President-Elect for the Unit. He has always been part of the high achieving team of CREDAI MCHI Kalyan Dombivli Unit in the past 10 years. Under his tenure as President, this year The Kalyan Dombivli Unit is celebrating a decade of completion! Social obligations are also not far behind for Mr Shitole. He is also member of Rotary Club of Kalyan. He has been supporting social activities for last 20 years from this credible NGO.

Mission

In the tenure as President Mr Shitole’s mission is to take CREDAI MCHI KDU at next level with a new zeal and vigour giving a new direction to the industry in Kalyan Dombivli MR. As a leader, he gives all credit to the tireless efforts and workings to the excellent team of managing committee who are always here to serve industry through a planned approach to achieve defined goals & objectives. Key agenda is -

  1. To finalize tasks like preparing a manual for Ease of Doing Business, preparing fire manual, effective implementation of Auto DCR in the KDMC, Awareness drive for new Unified Development Control Regulation for members and also other policy issues in relation to developmental aspects in the city connected to the various regulations of various authorities like SRA, MMRDA, MHADA etc.
  2. Formation of Task force and assigning tasks to Young team members as task leaders and senior members as guiding members for accomplishment of tasks with the involvement of architects, municipal authorities, technical consultants. Creating online portal as mymchi.com for the members for any assistance through the secretariat for issues faced. Providing knowledge based information etc.
  3. Creating Online Digital platform for Exhibitions for the developers in Kalyan Dombivli & MMR region. Also putting efforts through such platforms for creating demand in the industry and create consumer oriented image in the industry.
  4. Taking initiatives for City and Transit Oriented development by participating in the follow-ups with infrastructure developmental departments like MMRDA, Metro Corporation, PWD, UD, etc. to enchance the pace of infrastructure development in Kalyan Dombivli & MMR region. CREDAI MCHI is also actively been participating in initiatives of local development with KDMC like beautification of roads center verges, development of gardens etc.
  5. To expand Membership base of CREDAI MCHI KDU in certain areas of MMR like 27 villages and areas in the vicinity to Kalyan Dombivli boundaries.
Complete focus & dedication for adding value to the industry is core objective and mission.
